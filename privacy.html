Privacy Policy

Effective 30 June 2026 · Derive (“we”, “the app”)

Derive is a tool that lets a creator publish short-form videos they have made to the creator’s own connected social media accounts (Instagram, TikTok, and YouTube). This policy explains what data the app accesses, how it is used, and your choices. The app is operated by an individual creator and is used to manage that creator’s own accounts.

Information we access

When you connect an account through a platform’s official login (OAuth), the app requests only the permissions needed to post on your behalf:

  • Basic profile information (e.g. your account ID, username/handle, and avatar) — used to confirm which account a post will be published to.
  • Content-publishing permission — used to upload and publish a video you have chosen, to your own account, when you instruct it.
  • For YouTube, read-only channel information used to identify your channel before upload.

We do not collect your password. We do not read your private messages, your followers, other users’ content, or analytics beyond what is needed to confirm a post succeeded.

How we use it

Accessed data is used solely to publish the videos you create to the accounts you connect, and to show you the status of those posts. We do not use it for advertising, profiling, or any purpose unrelated to the posting feature you requested.

Storage & security

Authorization tokens for your connected accounts are stored locally in the operator’s own environment and are used only to call the official platform APIs on your behalf. Tokens are never displayed, logged in plain text, or transmitted to any third party.

Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your information with third parties. Data is exchanged only with the official APIs of the platform you are posting to (Instagram/Meta, TikTok, YouTube/Google), as required to publish your content.

Google / YouTube API — Limited Use

Derive’s use of information received from Google APIs adheres to the Google API Services User Data Policy, including the Limited Use requirements. YouTube data is used only to upload videos to your own channel at your direction; it is not transferred to others except as needed to provide that feature, and is not used for ads or sold.

TikTok

Derive uses the official TikTok Login Kit and Content Posting API in accordance with the TikTok Developer Terms and platform policies. TikTok data is used only to publish your own content to your own account at your direction.

Retention & revoking access

You can revoke the app’s access at any time in your account settings — Instagram/Facebook (Apps and Websites), TikTok (Security → Manage app permissions), or Google (Account → Security → Third-party access). Once revoked, the app can no longer access your account, and stored tokens for that account stop working and are removed.

Changes

We may update this policy; material changes will be reflected here with a new effective date.
