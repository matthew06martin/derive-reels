Derive

A tool for creating and publishing short-form study videos.

Derive helps a creator produce vertical short-form videos (study/“day in the life” style reels) and publish them to the creator’s own connected social accounts — Instagram, TikTok, and YouTube Shorts — from one place.

What it does

You connect your own social accounts via each platform’s official login. Derive then lets you post a finished video you created to those accounts on your instruction. It does not browse, scrape, or interact with anyone else’s content, and it only ever acts on the accounts you personally connect and authorize.

Works with YouTube

Derive publishes videos to your own YouTube channel (as YouTube Shorts) using the official YouTube Data API, and also to TikTok and Instagram. Use of YouTube data is governed by the YouTube Terms of Service, the Google Privacy Policy, and our own Privacy Policy.
